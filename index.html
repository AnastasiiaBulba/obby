How to Play

In Obby Rescue, your goal is to outsmart traps and save every crew member. Swipe to move, avoid hazards, and use your wits to solve each level. Every decision can mean the difference between rescue and disaster!

Controls

  • Swipe to move your Obby
  • Trigger rescue gadgets to clear obstacles
  • Collect keys and unlock trapped friends

Tips & Tricks

Gadgets & Tools

Master the essential rescue equipment that will help you overcome any obstacle and save your crew. Each gadget has unique properties and strategic uses!

Laser Disruptor

Temporarily disable security lasers and energy barriers. Perfect for creating safe passage through heavily guarded areas.

Range: Medium Duration: 10s

Magnetic Grappler

Pull yourself to metal surfaces and swing across gaps. Essential for navigating complex industrial environments.

Range: Long Cooldown: 3s

Shield Generator

Create a protective barrier that blocks hazards and traps. Use strategically to protect yourself and your rescued friends.

Duration: 15s Radius: Large

Time Warp Device

Slow down time temporarily to plan your moves and react to fast-moving obstacles. Perfect for precision rescue operations.

Duration: 8s Slow: 50%

Universal Key

Unlock any door or container to access hidden areas and rescue trapped crew members. Limited use but invaluable.

Uses: 3 Rarity: High

Emergency Beacon

Signal for help and reveal the location of all trapped crew members. Essential for completing rescue missions efficiently.

Range: Global Duration: 20s

Pro Tip:

Combine gadgets strategically! Use the Time Warp Device with the Laser Disruptor for perfect timing, or combine the Shield Generator with the Emergency Beacon to protect your rescued crew while signaling for help.
